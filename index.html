About Me

Hi! Thanks for stopping by my page. I'm Jane, a third year Game Design & Playable Media Major at the University of California, Santa Cruz. My focus in games highlights Production and Narrative Design; I have passion for executing impactful storytelling across a visual medium and helping creative projects make it over the finish line. I'm currently affiliated with UCSC's Game Design & Art Collaboration as the Mock Studio Coordinator, leading and managing the production of our annual student-developed game, with a rigorous development cycle of sixteen weeks. I work closely with a team interdepartment producers to faciliate studio goals and enable our creative team to do their best work.

Our current Mock Studio work in progress for 2022-2023 is TacoCat.
